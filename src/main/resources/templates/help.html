Help & Support

💬 How do I start a chat?

Go to the Chats page and select a user to start messaging them.

🖼️ How do I change my profile picture?

Go to the Settings page and upload a new image.

❌ How can I delete a chat?

Open the menu next to a chat and choose "Delete Chat".

🔒 Is my data secure?

Yes, your messages are encrypted and protected with secure authentication.

🌐 Can I use the app offline?

You can read existing messages offline, but sending or receiving new messages requires internet connection.

🚫 How do I block a user?

You can block a user either from the Privacy Settings page or from your Blocked List in Settings. Once blocked, the user will no longer be able to contact you.

✅ How do I unblock a user?

Go to your Blocked List in Settings, find the user, and click "Unblock" to allow them to contact you again. Users can also be managed from the Privacy Settings page.
